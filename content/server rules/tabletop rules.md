- Follows TTR Community [rules and guidelines](/server-rules).
- Dungeon/Game masters reserve final judgement on any rules, variations, or other content within their campaigns.
  - Players may bring up suggestions or possible corrections but are in no means permitted to override GM's decisions.
- Please refrain from speaking over others, your voice is not the only one within the story.
- Please respect any additional rules, guidelines, or other constraints asked of you by the current Dungeon/Game master in their campaign.
