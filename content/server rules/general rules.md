## General Rules

- All members are expected to treat others with common decency and respect.
  - You don't need to always play nice or bend over backwards but malicious or purposeful disrespect will not be tolerated.
  - Playful banter between friends is allowed, however if asked to tone it down or stop please respect all parties involved.
- Hate speech, slurs, or purposefully malicious speech against anyone is not permitted.
  - Discriminatory speech or comments based on someones sex, sexual preference, race, and etc. will result in an imediate ban without option for appeal.
  - Malicious speech intended only to harm another will result in an imediate ban without option for appeal.
  - General harassment, doxxing, etc. will result in an imediate ban without option for appeal.
- Members are expected to reconcile issues with others as they arise.
  - Members should be forth a best effort to discuss or otherwise work out the issue with all parties involved.
    - If uncomfortable doing so members may ask a third party to mediate if required.
    - For more serious matters please contact server staff (helper, staff, admin roles).
- When available streaming, ranked, or otherwise limited voice chats are open for all to use.
  - Please be considerate of those currently utilizing these rooms and follow their wishes within reason.
    - If someone is streaming they may request you leave the voice chat. Please do so if asked.
- Advertising other servers, guilds, communities, and etc. without staff permissions is not permitted.
  - If unsure please reach out to staff to confirm.
- Members are not required to be active but inactivity may result in temporary removal from community servers at staff discretion or you may be caught in the server prune options.
  - Generally when using the server prune options 30+ days of inactivity will result in removal from the server.

## Staff Specific

Staff are held to a higher standard then general members within our community. All staff members are required to follow all community and server rules for servers they are given permissions. Staff are also required to act in an unbiased fashion when asked to review issues with other members or when acting in response to rule violations. If it is found you are allowing violations/lax punishments for friends, or acting more harshly towards members you may not enjoy as much all roles will be removed from your account. You may also be kicked or banned based on the severity of the actions taken.

## Punishments and Appeals

### How punishments are handled.

In general punishments are doled out at the discretion of server staff and in most cases are final. If you feel you are being unfairly targeted by a staff member please reach out to server admin to resolve the issue. Screenshots or detailed descriptions are highly requested if this does occur.

#### Timeout

Staff may decide to utilize the timeout option should the situation call for it. The time of the timeout is decided based on the severity of the issue.

#### Muting/Deafen

Staff may mute or deafen you in community voice channels at time based on their discretion. Generally this is handled based on the situation at hand and may not require violation of community rules.

#### Kicking

You may be kicked by server staff for violation of any of the community rules. This could include removal from all community servers based on the severity of the issue. Generally if kicked you may rejoin community servers, however it may be best to wait a bit for things to cool cown.

#### Banning

Staff may ban if you are frequently violating community rules, causing issues with other members, or the servity of the rule violation or problem warrants immediate banning. Based on the severity of the issue you may be given the option to appeal your ban. If appeal is allowed and staff is able to DM you (based on your discord settings), they will provide you with this [form](<https://forms.talkingroom.xyz/forms/talking-room-uban-request-su8pcp>) to appeal your ban.

Appeals are determined based on the severity of your actions and your responses within the appeal form. Generally multiple staff members will consult to determine the result of your ban appeal.
