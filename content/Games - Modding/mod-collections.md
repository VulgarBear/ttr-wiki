We utilize a few different ways to mod our games including steam workshop collections, r2modman packs, and prism launcher. If you would like a mod to a pack below please speak to the owner. To have your pack listed here please contact server staff.

### Steam Collections
[Civ VI: Unified Collection](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2804180095): Collection of mods to enhance and improve on the default Civ VI playthrough. Generally used within our multiplayer games. (Vulgarbear)

### r2modman
r2modman packs are shared via codes. Generally an annoucement will be made if a pack has been updated. Check [here](https://wiki.talkingroom.xyz/game-modding/r2modman-install) to learn about installing the program.

**REPO**: `019670a1-132e-ff6d-0a5c-132c767a1971`

### Prism Launcher (minecraft)
Prism Launcer can be downloaded from their [website](https://prismlauncher.org/).

[Cobblin Creation](https://modrinth.com/modpack/cobblin-creation): Lightweight modpack focused on Create and Cobblemon.
